Navigation Bar

The Navigation Bar is located on every page, allowing you to log in, search a patient or medication, and also view what's in the cart.

Look-up or Add Patients

The patient button is located on the homepage. When hovering over the patient button, it will expand and give you the option to view an existing patient, or adding a new patient.

Look up Medications

The medication button is located on the homepage. When hovering over the button, you will be able to select four different options: Action, Oral, Injectable, and Inhalable depending on the form of medication you are looking for

Creating and Viewing All Patients

When clicking New Patient from the homepage, it will redirect you to this form. Fill out accordingly and click Save to add it to your current patients, or you may cancel.
When clicking Current Patient from the homepage, it will bring you to a list showing the patients Full name and Date of Birth, clicking a patient will redirect you to their information.

Patient Information

After clicking a patient from the list, you will be able to see the patients: name, picture, personal information (example of personal information shown below), health information, and records. You can add a comment that will be saved in the records by date. If you would like to remove a patient from the list, you can click the red button under the comment box that says, "remove patient".

Class Medication

Depending on the form of medication you clicked form the homepage, it will display the classes, to help navigate through a certain medication easier.
Once you click on a class, you will see a list of medications to choose from.
After clicking the medication, you will see a generic name, the class, price dosage, quantity, and a link that have a more detailed description of the medication. If you would like the prescribe the medication to the patient, click the red button that says "Add to Cart" and a message will pop up to alert you that the item has been placed in the cart.

Filtering Drugs

If you would like to search all medications from the class page, you can filter by the action depending on the patients needs.
You can also filter by the form of medication.
Also, the class of medication.
Lastly, you can filter by price if the patient has a certain amount they would like to stay in.

Log in

From the navigation bar, you are able to log in or register for an account.

Cart

After selecting the medications for the patient, you can check the cart page from the navigation bar to review all the medications and the total. Once reviewed, to checkout, click the blue button at the bottom of the page that says "checkout".
